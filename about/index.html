I research topics in global health, international development, and climate change at Rethink Priorities, a consultancy and think tank affiliated with the effective altruism movement. In practice, my team has focused on cause- and intervention-level analyses commissioned by charitable foundations and individual philanthropists.

I also offer English copy-editing services in line with rates set by the Chartered Institute of Editing and Proofreading of the United Kingdom. Please send me a message here if you’d like to work together.

While I’m primarily based in London, I’ve also studied and worked in the United States and China. In 2022, I graduated summa cum laude and Phi Beta Kappa from the University of Chicago, with a bachelor of science in environmental science with honors.
